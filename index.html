Agreement Terms

• The Borrower agrees to repay the loan amount in full on or before the specified due date stated in this Agreement.

• Any late payment shall result in a penalty of ₱50 per day until the loan is fully settled. Also, borrowers who have a record of delayed payments of 3 days or more with Good Payer Priority (GPP Lending) will no longer be allowed to borrow again.

• The Borrower must present at least one (1) valid government-issued ID upon application.

• The Borrower must be 18 years old and above to qualify for a loan.

• Failure to pay the total loan amount by the due date will authorize the Lender to:

i. Report the Borrower to his/her barangay of residence.

ii. Contact the Borrower’s listed character references and guardian for collection purposes.

iii. Publicly post the Borrower’s name and details as a delinquent debtor to warn others against non-payment.

• By signing this Agreement, both the Borrower and the Lender confirm that they have fully read, understood, and agreed to all the terms and conditions outlined herein.
